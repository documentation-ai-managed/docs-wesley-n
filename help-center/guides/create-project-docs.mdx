## Quick Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your dashboard">
    Click "Dashboard" in the top menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add New Project">
    Press the "New Project" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in details">
    Name your project and add a short description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create sections">
    Use "Add Section" to outline your docs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start writing">
    Click a section and begin drafting your docs.
  </Step>
</Steps>
